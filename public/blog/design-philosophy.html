The Intersection of Brutalism and UI

March 15, 2024

In a world of rounded corners and soft shadows, we choose the raw integrity of the grid. Digital brutalism isn't just an aesthetic; it's a rejection of the superfluous.

When we designed Ownedge, we looked back to the terminal interfaces of the 80s and 90s. Not for nostalgia, but for their efficiency. They respected the user's intelligence.

Core Principles

  • Function over decoration
  • High contrast for clarity
  • Response times measured in milliseconds

This path allows us to build tools that feel like extensions of the mind, not just apps on a screen.
